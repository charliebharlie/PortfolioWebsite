getPasswordCrackingproject()

In my project, I focused on optimizing password cracking strategies by implementing a Binary Search Tree (BST) approach. To achieve this, I leveraged leaked password lists to identify frequently used passwords and determine their strength. By organizing the passwords into a BST and employing three distinct criteria for ordering the tree, I aimed to enhance the efficiency of the cracking process. This approach allowed me to prioritize the most common and stronger passwords, thus increasing the likelihood of gaining unauthorized access to accounts and systems. The project aimed to develop a more effective and targeted method for password cracking.
